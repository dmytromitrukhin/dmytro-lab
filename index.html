About

Web laboratory

This website is being developed to bring together projects that help people solve non-standard tasks using only a smartphone. A desktop version of the website is also available and fully functional.

I have been working in the printing industry since 2006. My educational background includes graphic design and aircraft engineering. My experience combines print production, small-scale production automation, web applications, software tools, computer repair, hardware upgrades, operating systems, photography, and printing.

I create practical programs and web-based tools designed to simplify real-world workflows, improve efficiency, and make technical solutions accessible from everyday devices.

I am also interested in understanding how nature, the environment, and the human mind work — including consciousness, subconscious processes, and the connection between people and the world around them.
